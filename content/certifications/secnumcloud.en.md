# SecNumCloud

SecNumCloud is a security label issued by the French National Cybersecurity Agency (ANSSI). This certification guarantees a high level of security and data protection for cloud services.

## What is SecNumCloud?

SecNumCloud is ANSSI's reference framework for qualifying cloud computing service providers. It defines the security requirements that a cloud computing offering must meet to be qualified.

## Certification objectives

- **Digital sovereignty**: Ensure data remains under French/European jurisdiction
- **Enhanced security**: Protection against cyber threats and unauthorized access
- **Regulatory compliance**: Meet the requirements of sensitive administrations and businesses
- **Transparency**: Visibility on data locations and subcontractors

## Main requirements

1. **Data location**: Hosting in France or the EU
2. **Authorized personnel**: Administrators of French or European nationality
3. **Traceability**: Comprehensive logging of access and operations
4. **Encryption**: Protection of data at rest and in transit
5. **Reversibility**: Guarantee of data recovery
6. **GDPR compliance**: Compliance with European data protection regulation

## Qualification levels

SecNumCloud offers different levels depending on data sensitivity:
- **Standard**: For sensitive unclassified data
- **Enhanced**: For data requiring enhanced protection
