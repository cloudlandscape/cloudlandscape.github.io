netcup is a German hosting provider offering cloud solutions, VPS, and dedicated servers at very competitive prices. The netcup Cloud range includes cloud servers (VPS Cloud), S3-compatible object storage, additional storage volumes, and backup options. Particularly popular among developers and small businesses, netcup stands out for its excellent value for money, support in German and English, and datacenters located in Germany and Austria, ensuring GDPR compliance and data protection according to European standards.
