Hetzner is a German hosting provider recognized for its competitive pricing and high-quality infrastructure. Historically specialized in dedicated servers, Hetzner has developed Hetzner Cloud, a cloud computing offering that includes virtual instances (Cloud Servers), S3-compatible object storage, block storage volumes, and private networks. While simpler than hyperscalers, Hetzner attracts users with its transparent pricing, high performance, and datacenters located in Germany and Finland, ensuring GDPR compliance.
